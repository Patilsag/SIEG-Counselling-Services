# Sieg — Voice-Over Reel Scripts (faceless)

**How to make these without filming your face:**
- **Option A — AI avatar:** paste the VO SCRIPT into **HeyGen** or **Captions AI**, upload your voice clip + avatar → it renders the avatar speaking in your voice.
- **Option B — Voice + b-roll:** record the VO SCRIPT in your own voice, lay it over the B-ROLL shots, add the ON-SCREEN captions. No face, no avatar.
- Keep it ~30–40s. Hook must land in the first 2 seconds. Captions always on.
- Brand: navy/gold, logo bug bottom-corner, gold subtitles.
